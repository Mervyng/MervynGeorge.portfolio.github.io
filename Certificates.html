Certifications

Here are some of the certifications I have earned in my journey as a Creative Computing student. These courses have helped me build a strong foundation in web development and design.

INTRODUCTION TO ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

I earned this certificate through LinkedIn Learning by completing a comprehensive course that introduced the foundational concepts of AI. The course covered key topics such as machine learning, neural networks, natural language processing, and real-world applications of AI. I actively engaged in video lessons, quizzes, and practical examples to build a solid understanding of how AI technologies are shaping various industries.

CREATING A RESPONSIVE WEB DESIGN

I earned this certificate by completing an in-depth course focused on building websites that adapt seamlessly to different screen sizes and devices. Through hands-on projects and guided lessons, I learned how to use flexible grids, media queries, and mobile-first design principles to ensure a consistent user experience across desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

IT SECURITY

I earned this certificate by completing a structured course that covered essential principles of information security. The training included topics such as network security, threat detection, encryption, and best practices for protecting data and systems. Through real-world scenarios and assessments, I gained a practical understanding of how to identify and mitigate security risks in modern IT environments.

THE EVOLUTION OF THOUGHTFUL ONLINE SEARCH

I earned this certificate after completing a course that explored how search engines have evolved to deliver more relevant, intelligent, and ethical results. The course emphasized critical thinking, keyword strategy, search intent, and the role of algorithms in shaping online information. It helped me become more effective at conducting research and evaluating the credibility of digital content.

Trends That CHANGED FULL-STACK DEVELOPMENT

I earned this certificate by completing a course that highlighted the major shifts in full-stack development practices. The course covered the rise of JavaScript frameworks, serverless architecture, DevOps integration, and the growing importance of APIs and microservices. It provided insights into how modern tools and technologies are reshaping the way developers build scalable, end-to-end applications.

What is Generative AI

I earned this certificate by completing an introductory course that explained the fundamentals of generative artificial intelligence. The course covered how generative models like GPT and diffusion models create text, images, and other media. It also explored real-world applications, ethical considerations, and the future potential of AI-generated content.
