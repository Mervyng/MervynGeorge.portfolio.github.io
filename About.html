Biography

Hello! I’m Mervyn George, a second-year Creative Computing student at Bath Spa University RAK Academic Center. I’m passionate about blending creativity with technology to build engaging, user-centered digital experiences.

Alongside my studies, I’ve developed an interest in enterprise innovation, digital transformation, and people-centric leadership. I’m fascinated by how emerging technologies like cloud computing, AI, and data-driven design can solve real-world challenges and improve business outcomes.

My professional interests are supported by project experience and research in both front-end development and strategic tech thinking. I aspire to work in roles that allow me to simplify complex problems, collaborate across disciplines, and design practical solutions that scale.

Outside the classroom, I enjoy speaking with mentors in the industry, following ethical innovation trends, and exploring how technology can be used to empower people and communities.

Looking ahead, my goal is to contribute meaningfully to the tech industry through continuous learning, creativity, and a strong foundation in both design and strategy.

Personal Interests

Outside of academics, I’m a firm believer in lifelong learning. I enjoy exploring topics at the intersection of technology and society, especially how digital tools can improve education, sustainability, and accessibility.

In my downtime, you’ll find me reading tech blogs, watching tutorials, mentoring peers, or sketching UI/UX ideas for potential web projects. I’m also passionate about travel, which gives me a fresh perspective on collaboration, design, and global digital culture.

Above all, I value curiosity, empathy, and meaningful collaboration—whether through class projects, self-initiated ideas, or broader community work.
